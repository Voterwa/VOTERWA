About VoteRwanda

Our Story

VoteRwanda was created to simplify and secure the electoral process across Rwanda’s schools, business and Associations, and national elections. Born from a vision to empower every citizen with a trusted digital platform, VoteRwanda brings innovation and transparency to voting.

Our Mission & Values

We strive to promote democratic participation through technology, ensuring fairness, security, and accessibility for all voters. Our values include integrity, inclusivity, transparency, and innovation.

Our Vision

To become Rwanda’s leading digital election platform by continually innovating and delivering reliable, accessible, and secure voting solutions that foster democratic engagement.

A Message to Our Users

We thank you for trusting VoteRwanda. Our commitment is to provide you with a seamless voting experience where your voice matters. Together, we can build a stronger democracy.
