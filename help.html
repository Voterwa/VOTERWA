Welcome to VoteRwanda Help

We are here to assist you with any questions or problems you may have while using the VoteRwanda platform. Please find below useful information, frequently asked questions, and how to reach us.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • How do I register to vote?
    You can register through your school, workplace, or local election office depending on the election type.
  • Can I change my vote after submitting?
    No, once your vote is submitted, it cannot be changed to ensure fairness.
  • Is my vote confidential?
    Yes, VoteRwanda ensures that all votes are securely stored.
  • How do I view election results?
    You can view official results on the results page or from a leader who served you after voting closes.
  • Who can I contact for help?
